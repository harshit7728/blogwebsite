    Step 1 — Host your site online (free)

    Save this HTML file → drag it to Netlify Drop (free, instant live URL). Or push to GitHub Pages. Buy a .in domain (₹800/yr) from GoDaddy or Namecheap and connect it for a professional URL.

    Step 2 — Submit to Google Search Console

    Visit search.google.com/search-console → add your domain → verify ownership → request indexing. Google will start crawling your posts within days.

    Step 3 — Google AdSense Approval Checklist

    ✅ Publish 15+ original articles (500+ words each)
    ✅ Create About, Contact, Privacy Policy pages
    ✅ Use a custom domain (not a free subdomain)
    ✅ No copied/plagiarized content
    ✅ Site live for at least 2–4 weeks
    Then apply at adsense.google.com

    Step 4 — SEO Writing Tips for More Google Traffic

    🔑 Include city/area name (e.g. "Jodhpur") in titles and first paragraph
    📝 Target 600–1500 words per article
    🏷 Put your main keyword in title, first line, and meta description
    🔗 Link between your own articles (internal links)
    📸 Always fill Image Alt Text
    ⏱ Post 3–5 times per week consistently

    Step 5 — YouTube Videos = More Traffic

    Embed YouTube videos in your articles using the YouTube field in the editor. Articles with videos rank higher on Google and keep visitors on your page longer (great for AdSense revenue). You can embed your own videos or relevant public videos.

    Step 6 — Promote on Social Media

    Share every post on WhatsApp groups, Facebook local community pages, Instagram, and Twitter/X. Be consistent — local area Facebook groups and WhatsApp are the fastest way to grow traffic.
